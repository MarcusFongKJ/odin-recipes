Chicken Lasagne

Description

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Broth
  • Milk
  • Seasoning and herbs
  • Cheeses
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the onion and garlic in butter, then whisk in flour. Add the broth, milk and salt.
  3. Add some of the mozzarella and Parmesan. Season with basil, oregano and pepper. Set aside.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven.
